Faith-guided protection for families.

Every family deserves a safer internet that reflects their values. Sanctify gives religious families a simple, affordable way to filter harmful content without giving up privacy or learning a technical system.

How it works Get in touch

Protection should feel intentional, not invasive.

Sanctify is built around family authority, religious tradition, privacy, and technology that stays simple enough for everyday households.

Families come first

Filtering should help parents make intentional choices about the family's digital diet.

Faith traditions matter

What is appropriate varies by tradition. Sanctify is not a one-size-fits-all filter.

Privacy is sacred

Data is not sold, and individual browsing history is not shared with congregation leaders.

Technology should be simple

Families should not need a technical background to protect the screens in their home.

Communities can help

Spiritual leaders can offer support without turning accountability into surveillance.

Revenue should align

Participating organizations can receive recurring support from the families they help.

Protection before harmful content reaches the screen.

Sanctify blocks harmful content before it opens across computers, phones, tablets, smart TVs, gaming consoles, and home Wi-Fi. It works in the background without asking families to learn a technical system.

Filtering profiles are shaped with religious leaders and communities so each tradition can reflect its own values with care.

Specific browsing stays private.

Sanctify does not log or store the specific websites a family visits, does not sell data, and does not show individual browsing history to shepherds.

Never specific URLs shown to congregation leaders.
