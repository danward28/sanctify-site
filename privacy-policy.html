Sanctify ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Sanctify DNS content filtering service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and payment information processed securely by Stripe. If you join through a congregation, we also record that affiliation.

Device Information

For each device you register, we collect the device name, platform, and a unique device identifier. We also collect heartbeat data including app version, OS version, battery level, and DNS profile status to ensure your protection is active.

DNS Query Metadata

Our filtering service processes DNS queries to block harmful content. We record category-level metadata only, such as adult content was blocked, for compliance reporting. We do not log specific URLs or domain names that you visit.

IP Address and Location Data

We collect IP addresses from device heartbeats to detect geographic anomalies that may indicate account sharing. We do not perform precise geolocation. IP data is used only for abuse detection.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the DNS filtering service
  • To detect when device protection has been disabled or bypassed
  • To send compliance alerts to family administrators and shepherds
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To enforce household and device limits
  • To detect and prevent account sharing or abuse
  • To improve our service and develop new features

3. What Shepherds Can See

If your family has connected with a shepherd, such as a pastor, rabbi, imam, or other spiritual leader, they can see:

  • Your family name and number of devices
  • Device protection status, such as protected, degraded, or unprotected
  • Blocked content categories only, such as adult content or gambling
  • Compliance events, such as a DNS profile being removed

Shepherds can never see specific URLs, domain names, or browsing history. They see only aggregate category-level information to support your family's accountability goals.

4. Data Retention

We retain account data for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days. Heartbeat data is retained for 90 days. Compliance events are retained for 1 year. DNS query metadata, category-level only, is retained for 30 days. When you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days.

5. Device and Household Limits

Each Sanctify account supports up to 15 devices per household. One subscription is intended for use by one household at one physical address. We monitor device registrations, heartbeat patterns, and IP addresses to detect account sharing. If abnormal usage patterns suggest an account is being shared across multiple households, we may contact you or suspend the account.

6. Children's Privacy

Sanctify is designed to be used by families, including families with children. Account registration requires a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All device data associated with children is managed through the family administrator's account.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections, secure server infrastructure, and access controls. Payment information is processed by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AdGuard Home for DNS filtering infrastructure
  • Stripe for payment processing

Each operates under its own privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@sanctify.faith.
