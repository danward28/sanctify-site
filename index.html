Common questions

What families ask first.

What makes Sanctify different from ordinary filters?

Sanctify is built around AI-era threats and faith-shaped correction. It blocks dangerous destinations while keeping the blocked moment calm, private, and oriented toward formation.

Does Sanctify replace parenting or pastoral care?

No. It removes some of the constant technical exposure so parents and faith leaders can focus on trust, conversation, and formation.

Does the AI Portal include the filtering trial?

The 7-day trial applies to household filtering. Sanctify AI Portal is optional monthly access that starts when added because live model usage creates direct costs.

What is the refund policy?

The filtering trial is free for 7 days with no credit card required. After a family subscribes, paid filtering subscriptions include a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Can my faith leader see browsing history?

No. Shepherd support is by invitation. Leaders never receive URLs, searches, or browsing history from your household.

Which faith profiles are supported?

Sanctify supports Catholic, Protestant Evangelical, Mainline Protestant, Orthodox Christian, LDS, Jewish, Muslim, and additional faith expressions that can be expanded over time.
