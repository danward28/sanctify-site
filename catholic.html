Common questions

What Catholic families ask first.

Is Sanctify only for Catholic families?

No. Sanctify supports multiple faith traditions, but this page is configured for Catholic households and Catholic campaign traffic - the Catholic profile is selected for you at signup.

Which Catholic scripture sources appear on block pages?

Sanctify supports NABRE, RSV-CE, and Douay-Rheims. Your household chooses the source preference during setup.

Will my parish see what our family does online?

No. Parish leaders never see URLs, searches, browsing history, or household-level block details. Parish participation is a support and give-back model, not surveillance.

What devices are included?

iPhone, iPad, Android, Windows, Mac, Chromebooks, and smart TVs - plus your home Wi-Fi. Up to fifteen devices per household.

Does Sanctify block AI companion and deepfake tools?

Yes - dedicated categories for AI companion apps, deepfake and nudifier tools, adult AI services, anonymous chat, and sextortion-risk destinations, updated as new patterns appear.
